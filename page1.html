Mobirise

Blockchain logistics solution

Monitoring and control system for logistics companies, using RFID and blockchain technologies together

This technology developed for global logistics companies to help :

- monitoring and control cargo flows
- reduce cost delivery
- increase transparency of delivery
- flexible access to data
- reliable decentralized data storage 

  • For each delivery, a delivery contract is created in electronic form, in the form of a smart contract in the blockchain Ethereum
  • The delivery contract is created through the web form in the personal account of the participant in the system and contains all the necessary delivery parameters
  • The delivery contract is signed by the cargo owner and logistics company
  • The delivery contract allows to receive and store transactions related to delivery events in the blockchain.
  • The delivery contract interacts with the contract of transportations which contains all carried out and carried out transportations in the system
  • The contract of transportations contains the addresses of the delivery contracts and information on their status.
The key element of the system is RFID middleware, which, on the one hand, reads tag memory and determines the addresses of actual contracts , and on the other hand it interacts with a set of smart contracts, makes transactions about delivery events, relevant delivery contracts and a general contract of transportation.
